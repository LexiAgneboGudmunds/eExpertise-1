about us

We are eExpertise, our mission is to provide you and others who wish to learn more about computers with interactive lessons and tutorials,

Through endless hours of research and personal experience, we have come to the conclusion that the biggest problem for people looking to get into computers and technology is the large amount of knowledge required beforehand and the fact that learning everything by yourself takes an unnecessary amount of time; a google search won't always help as it can be misleading and may not be the answer you are looking for.

Therefore we have created this dynamic and interactive course, tailor-made to your needs.

our solution

To fix the problems that almost everyone faces as a beginner, we have created a course that is both informative and easy to grasp for everyone, from complete newcomers to people who are already well-versed in computers and tech.

Everyone is unique, and so is everyone's preference for what they want to acheive. By using our free course you will learn about the things in computer tech that matter to you and we promise to never send you any spam or promotions for products that are of no interest to you.

If your prefer to learn about computer hardware, you can for example be sent tutorials that explain the circuitry in motherboards or perhaps a detailed breakdown of chip-on-a-board computers and their uses. The options are endless, all on an easy-to-grasp level and without the complicated technical jargon. If you are more experienced in your niche then we will adapt to you and begin with more advanced lessons.

We are going to be upfront with you; we do have promotions of our own products in our course, but they would not be there if they were not beneficial to you and we will never force you to buy anything.
